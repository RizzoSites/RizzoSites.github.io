Elizé exclusive

Selected with uttermost scrutiny from a number of privately held and family-business suppliers, these blonde Russian hair extensions combine the exceptional look of true Russian hair, outstanding durability, and effortless application.

Here is the story: when we started working with Russian hair, we always picked the best. Without remorse we threw away suppliers that were not able to deliver the quality standard we set. After years, we managed to end up with a number of excellent Russian blonde hair providers, and a few superior ones, often small family businesses. The hair by these suppliers becomes the core of the Elizé Exclusive Russian hair collection.

If you aim to the finest, most precious and naturally looking Russian hair that is also durable and easily manageable – pick any of Elizé hair extensions. They’re the best. But if you just want more than that – Elizé Exclusive is for you.
