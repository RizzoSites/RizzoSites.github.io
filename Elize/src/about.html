About Elizé

Elizé was recently founded in 2018 in Turku, Finland. Prior to establishing ourselves in Finland, we have been in this business for four years, distributing hair from Ukraine directly to UK, US and German hair salons.

The mission of Elizé is to make it possible to get Slavic hair extensions in every part of the world easily while keeping the prices on the affordable range. We take care of each customer with maximum attention to every detail. Solid and long-lasting relations is what we aim to obtain with you.

Elizé maintains high quality by continually updating its list of suppliers. We cooperate closely with both Russian and Ukrainian hair factories to give out the product that matches today’s highest level professionals’ needs.

We never use ‘Remy, regular hair, nice fit, etc.’ as none of these Asian terms can be applied to our hair materials: Elizé products are always cuticle correct, each bundle gathered manually. Dying procedure is done very gently, usually, more than 12 times before we get the end product.

Collecting Slavic Hair
Slavic hair is highly reliable and long-lasting hair which can be easily worn in your daily life. The hair is thin, soft and suits various types of customers whose hair tends to have the same structure. Almost every type of fixation can be requested. The hair keeps having its softness when dyed at home or even bleached.
