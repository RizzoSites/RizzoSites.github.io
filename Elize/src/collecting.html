Selection and production of virgin Slavic hair

The real Russian hair producers do a little of refinement before the end product is released. Gathering hair is a hand-made procedure, where strands are selected thoroughly, combed and sorted to be cuticle correct to help Slavic strands to be resistant to getting mixed or tangled when brushed by the wearer.

Briefly, after clustering a portion of hair it goes through the disinfection to ensure the product contains none of the bacterias human’s natural hair may contain. Finally, the product is wrapped by an elastic band at the foundation of cuticles and offered as product in bulk hair for sale. Furthermore, depending on the type of connection, keratin capsules, tapes, wefts or clips are attached to the it.

Our each and every carefully selected supplier provides us with Slavic hair extensions gathered only in Slavic part of Russia and Ukraine. The process of gathering consists of the following stages:

1.

To begin with, the hairs are evaluated by the professional hairdressers: they check the health, the length, and the colour. Virgin Russian blonde hair bundles are sorted out first of all due to its incredible value.

2.

Donor’s strands are divided into four flocks that are tightly fixed by elastic band (this way, locks won’t fall down from the bundle) and then cut it. This is made in a extremely sterile conditions with clean high-end instruments. Sometimes, women visit a hair salon to have their hair cut by a professional, or, in rare cases, they may cut the stands by themselves and send the material by mail.

3.

Russian hair bundles are disinfected and washed several times with a hot water. It allows killing each persons’ own bacterias that remain on donor’s hair. At the final step of disinfection, cold water is used to remove detergent chemicals from the bundle.

4.

Hair flocks are dried in a special camera at the temperature of 40 °C. At this moment, the mass of mane can lose up to 20% of its weight.

5.

The hair sections are sent to the combing shop where masters user carding machines to comb the material. After the machine has combed the strands, the hand- made sorting starts. As inference, tangled combs that may have appeared during the previous actions are removed. Damaged and short hairs are also cleared away from the bundle at this time of processing.

6.

Combed hair flocks are put into large containers to be dyed. Special long-lasting dyes are used to make it possible for the masters to obtain various shades. To achieve the most brightest colours, Russian blond hair extensions are dyed with exceptionally delicate components causing the natural melanin to become bleached slowly and steadily.

7.

Finally, the material is washed in clean water, combed again and formed into the tresses of 100-200 g each. The strands of hair are stitched together into tresses in order to eliminate the risk of tangling and change of cuticle direction. Prior to dispatching, hair tresses are formed into bundles that are sorted by length and colour and are carefully packed in separate containers.

Elizé thoroughly selects among all the Russian hair suppliers in Russia and Ukraine to always make sure that we have the finest quality hair extensions.
